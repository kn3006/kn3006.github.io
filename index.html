About Me

About Me




I am a C-suite Executive, a Corporate Leader, who combines entrepreneurial spirit with leadership skills to drive business gains. A dedicated Leadership and Management skills trainer who believes that “Leadership and learning are indispensable to each other” and if one stops learning, he stops creating history and becomes history.

I brings with me a rich professional experience of over 34 years with reputed organizations like Raymond, Siyarams, Welspun, Creative Portico (New York) and Kiaana Home Originale – USA, with over 25 years in leadership roles spread over the domains of Marketing, Sales and Retailing.I am a trustworthy leader, a visionary, result oriented, strategically focused, comfortable at dealing with conflicts and making high-powered decisions. During my career I have developed, mentored and coached a number of young executives into successful managers and leaders.

I have successfully completed my Advanced Diploma in Training and Development with Indian Academy of Training and Development, Mumbai and I am now a man on a mission to pursue training with a purpose to share my knowledge and experience and help develop individuals and organizations.

Your inspiring Partner in Learning, Training and Development



Vision

My vision is to develop sustainable human capital for the future.

Mission

My mission is to design and deliver high impact training and development programs that produce outstanding human performance in your organizations. I provide companies with the core skills and knowledge to achieve high employee performance, competitive advantage and business success.

Guiding Philosophy

To create a strong and continuous improvement in human performance within your organization by creating a smart partnership with your organization to fully understand your unique business strategy, goals, needs, objectives and culture. Customize programs that are specially designed to build and sustain the skills and behaviours that are essential to your business success.

Commitment to you

To achieve your desired results, my development efforts extend beyond the training room. I will work in partnership with you to:


  • Understand your business and development needs
  • Design development initiatives and deliver high impact training programs
  • Develop and implement powerful reinforcement programmes
  • Track and measure for continuous improvement and sustainability


I firmly believes that Organizational growth and development overall is about meeting or exceeding objectives, increasing productivity, enhancing inter-departmental collaborations, and it is here where experiential training comes to aid. All of my training programs are designed with an objective of building competence and therefore experiential learning is an essential component of my training programs.
